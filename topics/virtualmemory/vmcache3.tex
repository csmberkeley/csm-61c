\begin{blocksection}
\question 
Now assume a context switch occurred and the cpu now runs the second program. Assume that the second program has run before, so after the context switch the cpu now holds the following TLB, with some uninformative columns omitted:
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{ |c|c|c| }
 \hline
 valid & tag & PPN \\
 \hline
 1 & 0xFFFFF & 0x001 \\ 
 \hline
 1 & 0x12345 & 0x000 \\ 
 \hline
 0 & 0xFFFFE & 0x000 \\ 
 \hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\begin{parts}
\part
0xFFFFF123

\begin{solution}[0.5in]
TLB hit \\
Physical address: 0x001123 \\
Compulsory miss
\end{solution}

\part
0x12345123

\begin{solution}[0.5in]
TLB hit \\
Physical address: 0x000123 \\
Compulsory miss
\end{solution}

\part
0xFFFFE123

\begin{solution}[0.5in]
TLB miss \\
Physical address: 0x002123 \\
Compulsory miss
\end{solution}

\part
0xFFFFF000

\begin{solution}[0.5in]
TLB hit \\
Physical address: 0x001000 \\
Compulsory miss
\end{solution}

\part
0xFFFFF1FF

\begin{solution}[0.5in]
TLB hit \\
Physical address: 0x0011FF \\
Cache hit
\end{solution}

\end{parts}

\end{blocksection}
