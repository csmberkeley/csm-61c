\begin{blocksection}
\question
You are given the following RISC-V code:

\begin{verbatim}
Loop:   andi t2 t1 1
        srli t3 t1 1
        bltu t1 a0 Loop
        jalr s0 s1 MAX_POS_IMM
\end{verbatim}

\begin{parts}
\part
What is the value of the byte offset that would be stored in the immediate field of the bltu instruction?

\begin{solution}[0.5in]
-8
\end{solution}

\part
What is the binary encoding of the bltu instruction? Please use hexadecimal to represent your answer. 

\begin{solution}[0.5in]
0xFEA36CE3
\end{solution}

\end{parts}
\end{blocksection}
